Influencer Marketing: Let's Grow Biogarden

Let's Grow bio fertilizers, a brand with a presence in 17 markets worldwide, aimed to expand into the German market, making it their 18th country. The company identified influencer marketing in the hobby gardening and self-sufficiency sectors as the most effective channels for sales and brand awareness

Consequently, Let's Grow sought out partners with expertise in influencer marketing within the German market to foster relationships with bloggers, enhance brand visibility, and drive initial sales
  • Developed a comprehensive influencer marketing strategy
  • Identified and analyzed over 100 potential bloggers, creating a robust database
  • Conducted a promotional mailing campaign to introduce the product to selected bloggers, allowing them several weeks to test its effectiveness
  • Launched the main campaign, ensuring regular postings with 5 bloggers each month
  • Provided weekly progress updates
  • Delivered detailed monthly analytics and performance reports
